Our Story

We envision a society where young people and women are empowered to realize their full potential

The Initiative for Awareness and Development (IAD) is a development- and social impact-driven organization that works with young people, women, supporters, and partners to create just, inclusive communities ensuring equity and opportunity reach every corner, all the way to the last mile.

To do this, we tackle the root causes of inequality and exclusion, working with young people, and women to amplify their voices, expand opportunities, and create lasting solutions. Our approach strengthens participation in decision-making, drives sustainable livelihoods, and nurtures wellbeing—ensuring equity and justice reach even the most remote communities.

Where it started:

2020

As the world stood still during the COVID-19 lockdown, a small circle of young visionaries met in the heart of Shauriyako, Homa Bay County Kenya. What began as an informal hangout in an informal settlement quickly evolved into a powerful idea: meaningful development begins with critical awareness. We saw a gap projects often arrived before people were truly prepared to own them. We believed the first investment must be in mindset: building informed, inspired communities ready to shape their own futures. Out of that conviction, Initiative for Awareness and Development (IAD) was born merging awareness and action, and driving change that lasts.

Our Strategy & Values

Our Strategy

At IAD, we work alongside young people, women, partners, and supporters to build communities that are just, inclusive, and resilient. We tackle inequality at its roots by amplifying voices in decision-making spaces, opening pathways to sustainable livelihoods, advancing health and education outcomes, and safeguarding the environment. Our approach is anchored on three pillars: Critical Awareness, Grassroots Solutions, and Strategic Partnerships.

Our Vision

A society where young people and women are empowered to realize their full potential.

Our Mission

To promote the welfare and involvement of young people and women in development through partnerships, capacity building and advocacy.

Our Core Values

At IAD, we are guided by five core principles the heartbeat of our work and the compass for our actions. Together, they form the acronym HEART, inspiring everything we do to create lasting change.

H - Humanity

Encompasses our capacity for compassion, kindness, and understanding towards others, acknowledging their emotions and experiences.

E – Equity

Embodies our principles of fairness and justice, ensuring that every individual, irrespective of their background or circumstances, has equal access to opportunities and fair treatment.

A - Adaptability

Our art of embracing change, being flexible, and adjusting to diverse situations. It involves resilience, an open mind, and the willingness to evolve in the face of challenges, fostering growth and progress.

R - Remarkability

Signifies our pursuit of excellence and distinction, going beyond expectations to make exceptional contributions.

T – Transparency

Involves our nature of being open, honest, and clear in both communication and actions. It's about freely sharing information, being accountable for decisions, and maintaining honesty in behavior, fostering trust and integrity.

Our Strategic Plan

In our 2021-2025 Strategic Plan, we have identified three core strategic priority areas that will guide our efforts:

Good Governance, Democracy & Human Rights:

Amplifying the Voices of Youth and Women: Empowerment in Action

At IAD! we steadfastly champion the tenets of good governance, and democracy recognizing them as the cornerstone of an equitable and inclusive society. Our unwavering dedication is centered on the empowerment of youth and women, fostering their active engagement in decision-making processes.

We ardently advocate for amplifying the voices of young people and women, seamlessly integrating them into the tapestry of local, national, and global policymaking. Our tireless efforts extend to advocating for the implementation of crucial policies and frameworks that foster the development of youth and women.

By equipping today's youth and women with the necessary tools and platforms to shape tomorrow's world, we are committed to paving the way for a more just and progressive society.
